About the event

Get a chance to hear from some of the most inspiring and interesting speakers from the creative fraternity across India on the 20th of May 2016 at Creative Foundry.

Creative Foundry is a ThoughtWorks initiative, a platform for some of the most creative minds in the field of design, and art and music to share, inspire and get inspired.

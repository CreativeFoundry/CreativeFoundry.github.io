About the event

Creative Foundry is a ThoughtWorks initiative to bring together creative minds in the field of art and design. Come along and enjoy an evening of a curated bunch of talks and experiences around the theme of “Creating for India”.

Between sessions and outside, you'll have lots of time to interact with other, like minded people.
